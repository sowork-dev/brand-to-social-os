# Example — Product Positioning

## User and use case
A founder wants to turn a rough company point of view into clear messaging and a two-week social plan.

## Job to be done
Create sharper narrative and publish consistently without sounding generic.

## Alternative used today
A mix of blank-page writing, disconnected prompts, and outsourced content that lacks strategic tension.

## Product promise
For founder-led teams, Brand to Social OS turns strategic ideas into a working content system without requiring a full agency layer.

## Why this is different
It connects positioning, messaging, execution, and review in one workflow.

## Launch angles
1. From vague brand to clear narrative
2. From product promise to weekly content system
3. From random posting to structured social operations
