# Social Operating System

Use this skill after brand and product positioning are defined.

## Objective
Turn positioning into a weekly system for publishing, engagement, repurposing, and learning.

## Inputs
- brand positioning
- product positioning
- target channels
- team size
- content resources
- business goal

## Workflow

### 1. Define channel role
For each channel answer:
- why does this channel exist?
- what audience behavior happens here?
- what kind of trust is best built here?

### 2. Build 3–5 content pillars
Recommended mix:
- education
- opinion
- proof
- behind the scenes
- conversion

### 3. Set cadence by team reality
Do not plan more than the team can sustain for 8 weeks.

### 4. Design the weekly loop
- Monday: topic planning
- Tuesday–Thursday: creation and publishing
- Daily: engagement and signal capture
- Friday: review and iteration

### 5. Define response expectations
Specify:
- reply SLAs
- comment triage
- escalation rules
- what counts as a meaningful conversation

### 6. Build the repurposing ladder
Example:
- one founder memo
- one long-form LinkedIn post
- one X thread
- three short takes
- one carousel
- one email block

## Output format

```markdown
# Social Operating System

## Goals
## Channel roles
## Content pillars
## Weekly cadence
## Engagement rules
## Repurposing flow
## Experiment backlog
## Weekly review questions
```
