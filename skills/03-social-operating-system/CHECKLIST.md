# Social Operating System - Pre-flight Checklist

Run this before building your social operating system.
A system built without clear positioning will produce noise, not compounding trust.

## Inputs ready?

- [ ] I have a completed brand positioning output (skill 01)
- [ ] I have a completed product positioning output (skill 02)
- [ ] I have decided which channels to prioritize (choose 1-2 to start)
- [ ] I know the realistic publishing capacity of my team for the next 8 weeks
- [ ] I have at least one person accountable for weekly review

## System design check

- [ ] Each channel in my plan has a defined role, not just presence
- [ ] My weekly cadence is honest about team capacity, not aspirational
- [ ] I have defined what counts as a meaningful engagement signal
- [ ] My repurposing ladder reduces creation effort, not just duplicate posts
- [ ] I have a Friday review ritual scheduled or it will not happen

## Red flags - stop and fix before continuing

- [ ] Planning 5+ posts per week on multiple channels with a 1-2 person team. Cut in half.
- [ ] No one owns weekly review. Assign before launch.
- [ ] Content pillars do not connect back to brand positioning. Realign first.
- [ ] Publishing plan exists but experiment backlog is empty. Add 3 experiments before week 3.

## After completing Social Operating System

- [ ] Save weekly calendar as weekly-social-calendar.csv
- [ ] Run first week before refining the system further
- [ ] Schedule Friday review as a recurring calendar event before you start
