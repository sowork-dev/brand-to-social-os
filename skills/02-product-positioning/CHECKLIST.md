# Product Positioning — Pre-flight Checklist

Run this after brand positioning is complete and before writing any product copy.

## Inputs ready?

- [ ] I have a completed brand positioning output from skill 01
- [ ] I know the specific product or feature I am positioning
- [ ] I can name the user segment this positioning is for (not "all users")
- [ ] I know the primary use case — what job they are hiring the product to do
- [ ] I can name what they are actually doing today without our product

## Product message clarity check

- [ ] My product promise names a specific outcome, not a feature
- [ ] My promise uses the frame: For [user], [product] helps them [result] without [tradeoff]
- [ ] My message hierarchy has one primary claim and 3 supporting claims
- [ ] Each supporting claim has at least one proof point
- [ ] I have addressed the 2–3 most common objections
- [ ] My launch angles are specific enough to generate content — not generic direction

## Red flags — stop and fix before continuing

- [ ] The product promise sounds like a feature announcement → reframe as an outcome
- [ ] The user definition overlaps with the brand audience but is not specific enough → narrow it
- [ ] The alternative is a fantasy competitor instead of what they actually use → be more specific
- [ ] There are no proof points, only claims → find evidence before continuing

## After completing Product Positioning

- [ ] Save output as `product-positioning-output.md` in your working folder
- [ ] Pass both brand and product positioning outputs to `04-content-pillar-design/SKILL.md`
- [ ] Use launch angles as the starting point for the first content batch
