# LinkedIn Operator Pack

Use after completing skills 01, 02, and 04.

## What LinkedIn is for

Professional trust-building. Audience uses it for learning, research, and vendor evaluation.
Content that teaches or shows expertise builds credibility over time.

## Inputs

- brand positioning output
- product positioning output
- content pillars
- post topic or raw idea

## Format guide

Long-form 150-300 words: education, opinion, proof, behind the scenes.
Short-form 50-100 words: quick takes, re-shares, experiment format.
Carousel: frameworks, step-by-step, before/after.
Poll: testing message angles, starting conversations.

## Rules

- First person throughout. "We" sounds like a press release.
- Hook line is the post. Weak hook means nothing else matters.
- Do not start with "I am excited to announce."
- Short paragraphs with generous line breaks.
- No links in post body. Put them in the first comment.
- Reply to every comment in the first 60 minutes.

## After publishing

Reply to every comment within 60 minutes.
Note comments that show strong resonance or objection.
DM anyone who asks a specific question worth continuing.
Add strong objections to experiment backlog.

## Repurposing ladder

Long-form post becomes: shorter same-week version, X thread, newsletter section, DM conversation starter.
