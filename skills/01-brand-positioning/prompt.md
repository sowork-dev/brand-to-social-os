# Brand Positioning — Claude Prompt

Copy this prompt and paste it into Claude. Fill in the [brackets] with your real information before running.

---

```
You are a brand strategist. Help me build a clear brand positioning using the following inputs.

Company: [your company name]
Category: [what category or market you are in]
Business model: [how you make money — SaaS, services, marketplace, etc.]

Primary audience: [who specifically you are targeting]
Buying trigger: [what situation causes them to look for a solution]
Current pain: [what they are frustrated or stuck with]
What they use today: [incumbent tool, manual process, workaround, or freelancer]

Founder or company point of view: [what do you believe is broken in this market?]
Proof points: [outcomes, credentials, process advantages, or lived experience]

Using these inputs, produce a complete brand positioning document with these sections:

## Audience
One specific sentence describing who this is for and what situation they are in.

## Core tension
What they are tolerating today that they should not have to.

## What they use today
The real alternative — be specific, not generic.

## Why that breaks
Why the current approach creates the problem we solve.

## Our strategic contrast
Complete this: "Most of this market still does ___. We believe the better model is ___."

## Positioning statement
One clear sentence: [company] helps [audience] [desired result] without [main tradeoff].

## Reasons to believe
Four to six specific proof points, not generic claims.

## Voice principles
Three to five tone and language rules that make content sound distinctly ours.

Quality bar: the output should be sharp enough that a content team could create 30 posts from it without sounding generic.
```

---

## Tips for better output

- Be specific in the audience field. "SaaS founders" beats "small businesses".
- Name a real alternative in "what they use today". Avoid "other tools on the market".
- The point of view field is the most important input. A weak POV produces a weak contrast.
- Run the output through `CHECKLIST.md` before passing it to the next skill.
