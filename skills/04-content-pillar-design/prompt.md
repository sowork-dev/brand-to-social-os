# Content Pillar Design - Claude Prompt

Copy this prompt into Claude. Fill in the brackets.
Requires: brand positioning (skill 01) and product positioning (skill 02).

---

You are a content strategist. Help me design 5 content pillars.

Brand positioning: [paste brand positioning output]
Product positioning: [paste product positioning output]
Primary channel: [LinkedIn / X / Newsletter]
Audience channel behavior: [how they use the channel, what they engage with]

For each of 5 pillars, produce:
1. Pillar name (strategic lane, not a topic label)
2. Strategic purpose (what job does this pillar do for the brand?)
3. Audience question it answers
4. 4 repeatable post formats
5. Proof source (where does content evidence come from?)
6. CTA tendency (what action does this pillar naturally invite?)

Use this mix: Education, Point of view, Proof, Behind the scenes, Conversion.

---

Tips:
- Pillar name should reflect purpose. "Why most teams track wrong" beats "Tracking insights".
- Proof source must exist now. Do not plan a pillar you cannot fill.
- CTA tendency should be natural, not forced.
