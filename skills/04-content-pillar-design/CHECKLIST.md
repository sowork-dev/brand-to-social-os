# Content Pillar Design - Pre-flight Checklist

## Inputs ready?

- [ ] I have completed brand positioning (skill 01)
- [ ] I have completed product positioning (skill 02)
- [ ] I know my primary channel and what audience behavior happens there

## Pillar design check

- [ ] Each pillar has a strategic purpose, not just a topic
- [ ] Each pillar answers a real question my audience is asking
- [ ] No two pillars overlap in purpose or audience question
- [ ] Each pillar has at least 3 repeatable formats
- [ ] Each pillar has a proof source I can draw on now, not later
- [ ] Conversion pillar is present but not dominant (max 1 of 5)

## Red flags

- [ ] All pillars sound like marketing, none like education. Rebalance.
- [ ] Proof source for a pillar is TBD. Do not publish from it yet.
- [ ] Pillar names are topic labels, not strategic lanes. Rename.

## After completing Content Pillar Design

- [ ] Add 2 post ideas per pillar before week 1
- [ ] Share with anyone creating content to align on tone and purpose
