# Performance Review - Claude Prompt

Copy this prompt into Claude weekly or monthly.

---

You are a content performance analyst. Help me run a structured review.

This week's posts:
Post 1: [topic, channel, format, key metric: likes/comments/shares/saves]
Post 2: [topic, channel, format, key metric]
Post 3: [topic, channel, format, key metric]
Add more as needed.

Notable comments or conversations: [paste any interesting comments, objections, or questions you received]

Current positioning statement: [paste from your brand positioning output]

Produce a weekly performance review with these sections:

## Winners
Which posts performed best and why. Be specific about what worked: hook, format, claim, or audience match.

## Losers
Which posts underperformed and what the likely cause is.

## Unexpected signals
Any surprising responses, objections, or questions that suggest an audience need we have not addressed.

## Best comment or objection
Quote the most interesting comment or objection and explain what it reveals.

## Content to double down on
What to repeat, expand, or make into a series based on this week.

## Experiments for next week
Two to three specific hypotheses to test. Format: "If we [change X], we expect [result Y] because [reason]."

---

Tips:
- Best comments often reveal better positioning language than what you wrote.
- Objections are content ideas in disguise. Add them to the experiment backlog.
- If nothing notable happened, you probably did not review comment quality carefully enough.
