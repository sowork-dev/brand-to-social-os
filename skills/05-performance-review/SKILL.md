# Performance Review

Use this skill weekly or monthly.

## Review dimensions

### 1. Message resonance
Which hooks, claims, or opinions got the strongest response quality?

### 2. Format-channel fit
Which ideas worked better as long-form, short-form, thread, carousel, or video?

### 3. Audience quality
Who responded?
- ideal customer
- peer operator
- low-fit audience
- passive engagement only

### 4. Business relevance
Which posts created:
- conversations
- leads
- invitations
- demos
- partnerships

### 5. Learning loop
Decide:
- what to repeat
- what to stop
- what to test next

## Weekly output

```markdown
# Weekly Performance Review

## Winners
## Losers
## Unexpected signals
## Best comments / objections
## Content to double down on
## Experiments for next week
```
