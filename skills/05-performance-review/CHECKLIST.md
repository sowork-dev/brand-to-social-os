# Performance Review - Pre-flight Checklist

## Before weekly review

- [ ] Data from at least 3 posts this week
- [ ] Reviewing comment quality, not just likes or impressions
- [ ] 30 minutes blocked without interruption

## Review quality check

- [ ] Noted which hook or claim drove the best response
- [ ] Identified at least one conversation that moved beyond surface engagement
- [ ] Noted which format performed best
- [ ] Documented what the audience pushed back on or disagreed with

## Red flags

- [ ] Skipping review because nothing happened. Look closer.
- [ ] Only tracking vanity metrics. Realign to conversation quality.
- [ ] No experiments planned for next week. Add at least one.

## After review

- [ ] Add at least one new experiment to the backlog
- [ ] Update content calendar based on what worked
- [ ] Share one insight with content team or collaborators
